About

Did you know that about 40 % of the food produced in India is wasted? Approximately 84.7% of the total food waste recorded was thrown in the bin.
There can't be the scarcity of food on the Earth it's only mis-management, which Our team is working to rectify and pave the way for a brighter future. Our goal is to intelligently connect the needy with his need, so as the hunger gets feed.

Indeed, food waste is a major issue in India, as well as in many other countries around the world. It is estimated that globally, one-third of all food produced is lost or wasted, which has significant economic, social, and environmental impacts.

In the words of Mahatma Gandhi, "Be the change you wish to see in the world." By working together, we can create a more equitable and sustainable future for all.
At our organization, we are committed to addressing this issue by working to reduce food waste and ensure that food is distributed to those who need it. Through partnerships with local farmers, businesses, and community organizations, we are able to rescue surplus food and redistribute it to food banks, shelters, and other organizations that serve those in need.

As you mentioned, small acts of kindness can make abig difference, and we encourage everyone to do theirpart to support their communities. Whether it'svolunteering at a local food bank or donating to ahunger relief organization, there are many ways tohelp ensure that everyone has access to the food theyneed.

Food Providers

Many peoples have excess food that is still edible but may go to waste if not used. By donating this food to the needy, food providers can reduce food waste while also helping to alleviate hunger in their community. Additionally, providing food to the needy can help to build a positive reputation for the food provider and increase their social impact.

Food For Needy

Our team aims to bridge the gap between those who have excess food and those who are struggling to make ends meet. We believe that everyone has the right to access basic necessities like food, and we are committed to making this a reality.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteering can be a great way to give back to your community, learn new skills, and meet new people. Whether you're looking to build your resume, gain experience in a new field, or simply want to make a difference, there are many reasons to consider volunteering.

Volunteering can also be a fun and rewarding experience. You might have the opportunity to work on exciting projects, attend events and fundraisers, and connect with like-minded individuals who share your interests and values.

If you're still not convinced, consider some of the benefits of volunteering:

  • Boost your resume or college application
  • Develop new skills and gain valuable experience
  • Improve your mental and physical health
  • Make new friends and expand your social network
  • Give back to your community and make a difference
